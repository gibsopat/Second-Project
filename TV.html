What's On TV?

Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice

I admire Batman V Superman: Dawn of Justice simply out of the uniqueness of the movie. I doubt this type of story will be adapted to the screen anytime soon, but aside from the movie itself is alright. The enjoyability definetly depends on the person, so if you are looking for a classic type of DC story, this isn't it.

Psych

Psych is a hilarious crime/cop tv show that's fun to watch, no matter the time or season. It has a new and interesting idea that's executed amazingly by a cast that plays their roles perfectly.
